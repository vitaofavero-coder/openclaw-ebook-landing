Chapter 3: Why Security Actually Matters

"I'm just automating emails, why do I need security?"

Here's why:

Your AI assistant has access to everything:

  • Your email inbox (including passwords reset links)
  • Your social media accounts (public reputation)
  • Your files and documents (potentially sensitive data)
  • Your calendar (your entire schedule)
  • Your messaging apps (private conversations)

A compromised assistant could:

  • Send spam from your accounts (destroying your reputation)
  • Leak private information publicly
  • Access financial data or credentials
  • Impersonate you in conversations
  • Delete important files or emails

Automation #1: Email Digest (Saves 5 hours/week)

The Problem: You check email 20+ times per day, context-switching constantly.

The Solution: Let your assistant monitor your inbox and send you a smart digest twice per day.

How to set it up:

  1. Connect your email to OpenClaw (Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  2. Tell your assistant:
Every day at 9am and 3pm:
- Check my inbox for new emails
- Summarize the important ones (meetings, urgent requests, deadlines)
- Send me a digest in Telegram
- Mark unimportant emails as read

The Result:

Instead of checking email 20 times, you check your Telegram digest twice. You respond to what matters, ignore what doesn't.
